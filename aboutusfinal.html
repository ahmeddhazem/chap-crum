About Us

Mission

At Chapters&Crumbles, we believe in creating a cozy sanctuary where the love of coffee, the joy of reading, and the art of baking come together. Our mission is to serve high quality, ethically sourced coffee and freshly baked goods, all while providing a welcoming space for book lovers to relax, explore, and connect. We strive to foster a community that celebrates creativity, imagination, and the simple pleasures of life, one cup and one page at a time."

Vision

Our vision is to be a cherished community hub, where every cup of coffee, every bite of pastry, and every page turned fosters a sense of belonging and encourages a love for reading and shared experiences. We aspire to cultivate a culture of creativity, conversation, and compassion, making our shop a place where memories are made.

Mayar Yasser

CEO

Mayar Yasser the heart and soul behind Chapters&Crumbles. With a lifelong passion for coffee, baked goods, and literature, She envisioned a space where these elements could come together to create a unique experience for the community.
